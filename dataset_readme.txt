The attached dataset contains rental payment information from a group of consumers. It contains the following fields and is comma delimited

name - The first and last name of the consumer
dob - The date the consumer was born
houseID - A key representing a single rental property
houseZip - The 5-digit zipcode of the rental property
paymentDate - The date the rental payment was made
paymentAmount - The amount payed in rent on the payment date
rentAmount - The amount of rent due
